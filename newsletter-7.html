Anthony Tam

Executive Chef

For each issue of Living Octave, we’ll be introducing one of our many talented team members. This issue we are proud to talk with Anthony, our Executive Chef. Born and raised in Macau, Anthony has recently resettled in Asia after working as a Chef for over 20 years in restaurants and hotels across the UK, Holland, and most recently, Azerbaijan.

He has created dishes and menus concepts, and trained kitchen teams in a plethora of cuisines including Japanese, Chinese, Thai, Italian, and French at renowned restaurants such as Annabel’s London, Hakkasan, and Nobu. Anthony is passionate about making dining fun, beautiful, and as healthy and environmentally conscious as possible.

Project Updates

Octave Living Room

After months of thoughtful planning, hundreds of sketches, and endless dedication, like proud parents; we are ecstatic to introduce our pilot project, The Octave Living Room (OLR), formerly known as the Shanghai Learning Center. The OLR is scheduled to be completed in March, 2014.

Suzhou Harmony

Octave's Harmony project has been expertly designed with the healthy family in mind. What is now a construction site will soon be a full-service, purpose-built community offering a wide variety of health and wellness related services. Our 69-room Resort will offer a range of stay packages designed for detox, rejuvenation and chronic illness recovery as well as reflection in the privacy of a luxurious setting.

East West Learning Center

East West Learning Centre (EWLC) is looking forward to the new year with excitement. With its mission of coupling organizational transformation with personal transformation, a key focus of the past six months has been recruiting new staff.  Three business processing consultants; one psychologist; and one talent management have been added to build EWLC's capabilities.
